Caroline's Foods & Beverages

Role: Full Stack Logic | Tech: JS, n8n Automation, E-commerce Logic
View Live Project ↗

The Challenge

Caroline's F&B expanded from a restaurant to a general trading depot (selling pure water and ice blocks). They needed a platform that could handle specific business rules: distinct minimum order quantities for different products, pricing that changes based on the Nigerian seasons (Rainy vs. Dry), and strict delivery zones.

The Solution

I built a robust e-commerce front-end with heavy JavaScript logic and automated backend processing:

  • Automated Seasonal Pricing: Wrote a JS algorithm that detects the current month. It automatically increases Ice Block prices during Dry Season (Nov-Mar) and lowers them during Rainy Season.
  • Constraint Logic: Implemented "Minimum Order" enforcement (e.g., minimum 10 bags of water) and "LGA Locking" which prevents checkout if the delivery address is outside Amuwo-Odofin.
  • Automated Orders (n8n): Instead of a static form, I integrated an n8n webhook that processes orders instantly, sending structured data to the business owner.
  • Bot Protection: Integrated honeypot fields to prevent spam orders.

The Outcome

The business now has a "self-managing" pricing system and a digital order flow that filters out invalid locations before they even reach the owner, saving significant administrative time.
